Research

I study how individuals’ characteristics and contexts play a role in how they think and learn.

Learning

  • Scaffolds and conceptual change in science (Medrano et al., 2020)
  • Perceptual cues (Medrano & Prather, in prep b)
  • External memory aids (Medrano & Miller‐Cotto, 2025)
  • Fading (Miller‐Cotto & Medrano, 2025)

Cognition

  • Executive functions in mathematical and science cognition (Medrano et al., 2025; Medrano & Prather, 2023; Medrano & Prather, in prep a)
  • Inhibitory control in arithmetic (Medrano & Prather, 2023)
  • Spatial skills and executive functions (Medrano et al., in prep )

Context

  • The effects of air pollution (Medrano et al., 2022)
  • Subgroup differences in Asian American children’s school readiness (Medrano & Miller‐Cotto, in prep )
